In today's society, in this transition stage, young people encounter barriers of many kinds, These barriers extend from the personal part, such as mental and psychological health. Going through situations that include the family, it may be due to pressure from parents and others. In even -and the most recurring-, situations where the field of action is lost, such as legislation and public policies.

Whatever type they are, something is very clear, young people need to have the tools to overcome them and achieve our goals.
